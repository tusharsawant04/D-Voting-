D-Voting Site

The D-Voting Site is a website which allows easy creation and management of online election

Create your own poll

You can easily create a poll using D-Voting Site and manage it in realtime. The other users you share the poll with will be able to vote and see the results at the end of the poll.

Making your own poll

1

Step 1: Create a account

Firstly you'll need to create a account to create a poll and also to vote to polls created by other user

2

Step 2: Creating your own poll

After creating your account you can login and open the user page. You can use Create a Poll button and provide relevant details and publish it.

3

Step 3: Vote

As you create a poll, a POll id will be provided which must be shared to other users. This Poll ID will be used to identify your poll

4

Step 4: Results

At last you can display the results of the poll to everyone who has access to it.
